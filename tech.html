The problem

Because of COVID-19, Barclays travel insurance is off sale. How do we increase our other insurance products profit by £1m to fill the temporary gap.

We have an existing product called Tech Pack, a packaged insurance for existing Barclays customers. It covers up to 4 mobile phones, unlimited gadgets and worldwide protection for loss and theft for £14.50 / month.
