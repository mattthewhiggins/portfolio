Design Lead – Barclays
(Sep 2015 – Present)

During my time working at Barclays there are 3 projects that I am mostly proud of.

CX Transformation which looked at stripping our digital offering back to its skeleton and building a concept bank. Doing the simple things very well and adding innovation. It was pitched to our CEO as our strategy to compete with new fintech companies looking to capitalise once once open banking happens.

Design lead and principal for Barclays design language which is a UI library designed and built for native app and responsive web. It allows Barclays to build anything digital at a pace never seen before at the bank. Barclays has 3 major brands within the group and we designed and built the library to have themable components, meaning for example a button is only needed to be built once, only the theme changes.

Finally Platforms, which was completely looking at changing how Barclays can pivot into a platform business. By Ideating and building products and services that connects the already existing huge network of individuals, SME'S and Coproate customers.

I've also enjoyed working on Barclays Launchpad which is a beta app that allows the design team to quickly get customer feedback on new ideas. One of my ideas that went on to being released was featured on BBC News - Technology﻿
