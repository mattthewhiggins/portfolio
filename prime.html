The Prime package doesn't hold itself together as a brand. It feels like a disjointed set of additional apps. It doesn't feel like a family of premium services.

In the digital age the biggest brand asset a company has is the app icon. So that’s where I suggest they start.

By simply using the palette from the logo you can create a suite of app icons to reflect the benefits of the subscription. People will know before having to download it, whether or not it's included with Prime.

Remove any wording from the app icons, simply because on your home screen on your phone or tablet you have the words under the icon and is unnecessary duplication.

Free and quick delivery is the #1 reason for people getting Prime, the second is Prime Video. But here you see it's ranked #4 video service in the UK and US.

Globally it’s ranked 5th.
